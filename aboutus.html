About GeoSearch

Geo-Search is a tool that allows people to search for places and extract information such as timezone, map and local weather conditions like temperature, wind speed, humidity, and precipitation.
Application web page(s) are fully responsive and optimized for a wide variety of form factors and Users can search for their respective places of interest.
More so, a geographical view a map of a place with a map marker identifying the location of the place of interest.
The local weather conditions (wind speed, temperature, humidity and the current weather condition) can be gotten from the app also.
Users can convert the temperature of any Location between Celsius and Fahrenheit metric system.
User can share a map of a place of interest or its local weather conditions to Facebook.
